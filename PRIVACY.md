# Privacy Policy for Increps

**Last Updated: November 8, 2025**

## Overview

Increps ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data from you.**

Increps does not:
- Collect personal information
- Track your usage
- Send analytics data
- Use third-party tracking tools
- Require an account or login
- Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
- Transmit any data over the internet

## Data Storage

All your exercise data is stored **locally on your device only**. This includes:
- Exercise names
- Repetition counts
- Workout history
- Application settings

Your data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not synced to any cloud service
- Is not shared with third parties
- Is deleted when you delete the app

## Third-Party Services

Increps does not use any third-party services, including:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

Increps does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages (4+).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue at:
https://github.com/ypapax/increps-support/issues

---

**In short: Increps collects zero data. Everything stays on your device.**
