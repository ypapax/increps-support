# Increps Support

Welcome to Increps - Exercise Counter support page!

## Getting Help

For support, bug reports, or feature requests, please [open an issue](https://github.com/ypapax/increps-support/issues).

## About Increps

Increps is a simple and intuitive exercise counter app for iOS that helps you track your workout repetitions.

### Features

- **Quick Rep Counting** - Increment and decrement controls with haptic feedback
- **Color-Coded Activity** - See at a glance when you last worked out
  - Green = Today
  - White = Yesterday
  - Yellow = 2 days ago
  - Red = 3+ days ago
- **Daily Progress Charts** - Visualize your reps per day with beautiful graphs
- **Smart History** - Workouts organized by day with collapsible sections
- **Hide/Unhide Exercises** - Keep your list focused on active exercises

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a new exercise?

1. Tap the **+** button in the top right corner
2. Enter the exercise name
3. Tap **Add**

### How do I track reps?

1. Open an exercise from the list
2. Use the **<** and **>** buttons to adjust the rep count
3. Tap **Add** to log your set

### How do I hide an exercise?

1. Open the exercise you want to hide
2. Tap the **eye-slash** icon in the top right
3. Confirm you want to hide it

### How do I unhide exercises?

1. Tap **Edit** on the main exercise list
2. Scroll to the **Hidden** section at the bottom
3. Tap **Unhide** next to the exercise you want to restore

### Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your workout data on external servers.
